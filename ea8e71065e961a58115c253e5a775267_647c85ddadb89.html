<p class="my-t-10 uppercase letter-spacing2">A mechanical workshop, also
known as an auto repair shop or garage, is a facility where mechanical repairs and
maintenance are performed on vehicles. It is typically equipped with specialized
tools, equipment, and skilled technicians to diagnose and fix various mechanical
issues in automobiles.</p>
<p class="my-t-10 uppercase letter-spacing2">Mechanical workshops are
equipped with a variety of tools and equipment to perform repairs and maintenance on
vehicles. Here are some common equipment found in a mechanical workshop:
Lifts: Hydraulic lifts or car hoists are used to elevate vehicles,
allowing mechanics to access the undercarriage for inspections and repairs. They come
in different types, such as two-post lifts, four-post lifts, scissor lifts, and
alignment lifts.
Diagnostic Tools: Diagnostic tools help technicians identify issues in
the vehicle's systems. They include onboard diagnostic (OBD) scanners, scan tools,
and computer software that can retrieve error codes, perform system tests, and
monitor sensor data.</p>
